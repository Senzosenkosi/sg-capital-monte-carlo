### How Idiosyncratic Risk is Calculated:

**Formula:**
```
Idiosyncratic Volatility = √(Total Vol² - Systematic Vol²)
```

**Example for CPI:**
- Total CPI volatility: 25%
- Systematic (explained by factors): 17.2%
- Idiosyncratic: √(25² - 17.2²) = **22%**

**Portfolio-level:**
```
Portfolio Idio Vol = √(Σ (weight_i × idio_vol_i)²)
                   = √((0.25×22)² + (0.25×18)² + (0.20×28)² + (0.15×32)² + (0.15×38)²)
                   = 11.72%
```
